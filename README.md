# Xenox - An AI powered arm assistant featuring vision (and maybe voice control!)

My idea is to make an assistant that can help me hold some parts or a fan when soldering, and do other tasks like give basic info/research with voice input, see plan below!

## Plan / Ideas
- Make the arm have 6 axis
- Claw with 3 (?) fingers
- Camera in the middle of the claw
- Microphone & Speaker for Voice control
    - AI Model self-made/Some open-sourced one
- Make an idle/sleep pose where the arm folds into itself (My desk is lowkey small)


## Description at submission time

Xenox is a robotic arm made to help me with research while desigining projects and possibly even the build process of said projects.

As you can see from the plan above, this project was something i wanted to make to help me do stuff on my desk/help with research and so on. As of right now, i have the full 3d model of the arm and a simple program to control it (probably, need to test). At first, ill just use simple voice commands but will definetly add an AI to this later on.

In short, i was inspired to make this by some Iron man clip i saw online, and i thought it would be extremely cool to have a robot companion/assistant sitting on my desk that could help me with research and soldering/building activities.

I think i made the arm decently close to my initial plan, only changing some stuff that turned out to be better off not added, like speakers and microphones, as i can use a headset as an alternative.
